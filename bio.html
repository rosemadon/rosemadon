Biography

Rose Madon is the penname of an author who lives in a moderately dry flat in New Zealand with a black cat and a very understanding wife-to-be. She is usually found thinking about aliens or vampires or fae or something horrible, and her writing always has an undercurrent (or deluge) of romance. She writes about lesbians because she is a lesbian and you can't improve on a perfect thing.
